In a Changing New York, Evictions are on the Rise

By: Mary Beech

December 22, 2025

In less than 10 days’ time, New York City will inaugurate its youngest mayor in over a century - a democratic socialist who, mere weeks before this year’s primary, was considered a long shot candidate. And while pundits may not have predicted his win, they tend to agree that he achieved it running first and foremost on affordability - an issue that came to dominate the mayoral race as a whole.
